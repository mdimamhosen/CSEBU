The Department of CSE at University of Barishal was established, marking the beginning of a journey in computing education.
2016: First Graduating Batch
Our first cohort of students graduated, setting a high standard for future generations.
2019: Research Lab Opened
Inauguration of the state-of-the-art research lab, boosting innovation and collaboration.
2023: National Award
Department received a national award for excellence in computer science education.
