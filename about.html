Introduction Of CSE-BU

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at the University of Barishal was established in 2016 with a vision to empower the next generation of innovators and leaders in computing. Our department is dedicated to providing a modern, research-driven education, fostering creativity, and preparing students for impactful careers in technology and academia.

What sets us apart is our commitment to hands-on learning, international collaborations, and a vibrant campus culture. With state-of-the-art labs, experienced faculty, and a focus on real-world problem solving, CSE-BU stands as a beacon of excellence in the southern region of Bangladesh.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Mission

To deliver high-quality education and research in computer science, equipping students with the skills, knowledge, and values to excel in technology and serve society.

Vision

To become a center of excellence in computing, recognized for innovation, leadership, and societal impact in Bangladesh and beyond.

Core Values

  • Ethics: We uphold integrity and responsibility in all our actions.
  • Innovation: We foster creativity and embrace new ideas.
  • Collaboration: We believe in teamwork and shared success.

Our Journey: History & Milestones

2016: Department Founded

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at University of Barishal is established, marking the beginning of a new era in computing education in the southern region.

2019: First Graduating Class

The first cohort of CSE-BU students graduate, stepping into the world as skilled professionals and innovators.

2021: Modern Labs Established

State-of-the-art computer and research labs are inaugurated, enhancing hands-on learning and research opportunities for students and faculty.

2023: National Award

CSE-BU receives a prestigious national award for excellence in computer science education and research.

2024: New Research Lab

A dedicated research lab is launched, fostering innovation and advanced study in emerging areas of computing.
